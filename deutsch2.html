📝 German Class Notes

📚 Vocabulary

das Buch – the book
die Katze – the cat
laufen – to run

🧠 Grammar Notes

Der, Die, Das: "der" is masculine, "die" is feminine, "das" is neutral.
Verb position: In a main clause, the verb is in second position.

🗣️ Phrases & Sentences

  • Wie heißt du? – What is your name?
  • Ich spreche ein bisschen Deutsch. – I speak a little German.
  • Woher kommst du? – Where are you from?
